Our Story

Started with a sourdough starter,
now we can't stop.

Crumble & Co. began in 2009 when Wanjiru Kamau quit her corporate job, dusted off her late mother's recipe notebooks, and started baking for neighbours from a two-burner kitchen in Kilimani.

Word spread faster than we could bake. Today we run a full production bakery with a small but deeply caffeinated team — still using the same slow fermentation methods and locally-sourced ingredients.

🌾
Local wheat, always We source from Unga Ltd and small Rift Valley farms.
🕓
Baked from 4am Everything is made the morning you buy it. No overnight warmers.
♻️
Zero-waste promise Day-old bread goes to Mama Oliech's kitchen in Kawangware.
